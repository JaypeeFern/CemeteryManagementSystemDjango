SURIGAO MEMORIAL PARK
Established 1987, A Private Cemetery in Surigao City, Philippines
About the Company
The Surigao Memorial Park is the first and only private cemetery in Surigao City. It is operated by Surigao Memorial Park, Incorporated, a SEC-registered company.
The SMPI is headed by a Board of Directors and run by professional managers who have carefully planned the daily upkeep, management and future direction of the park since 1987.
